## Getting Started

### Installation

1. Clone the repository to your local machine
2. Install dependencies:
   ```bash
   npm install
   ```
3. Start the development server:
   ```bash
   npm start
   ```
4. Open your browser and navigate to `http://localhost:4200`

### App Navigation

The app has three main sections accessible from the navigation menu:

- **Home** - View popular stocks at a glance
- **Search** - Search for specific stocks
- **Watchlist** - Manage your personal watchlist of stocks

## Search Functionality

The Search page allows you to find any publicly traded stock quickly.

### How to Search

1. Navigate to the **Search** tab
2. Enter a stock symbol or company name in the search bar
3. Results will appear automatically (up to 10 results)
4. Results include stock symbols and company names

### Working with Search Results

- **Add to Watchlist**: Click "Add to Watchlist" to save any searched stock
- **View Details**: Click on a result to navigate to the detailed stock information page
- **Refine Your Search**: Modify your search query to find different stocks

### Search Tips

- Use stock **symbols** for faster results (e.g., "AAPL" instead of "Apple")
- Search is case-insensitive
- Try partial matches to find multiple related stocks

## Watchlist Management

Your Watchlist is your personalized stock portfolio tracker.

### Adding Stocks to Your Watchlist

You can add stocks from two locations:

1. **From the Home Page**: Click "Add to Watchlist" on any popular stock
2. **From Search Results**: Search for a stock and click "Add to Watchlist"

### Viewing Your Watchlist

1. Navigate to the **Watchlist** tab
2. All your saved stocks are displayed here
3. Each entry shows the stock symbol and company name

### Benefits of Using the Watchlist

- **Quick Access**: View all your favorite stocks in one place
- **Persistent Storage**: Your watchlist is saved locally on your device
- **Organization**: Keep track of stocks you're interested in
- **Easy Comparison**: Compare multiple stocks side by side

### Removing Stocks

To remove a stock from your watchlist, navigate to the watchlist and use the remove option next to the stock you wish to delete.

2. **Use Search Efficiently**: Use stock symbols for faster, more accurate search results
3. **Build Your Watchlist**: Actively add stocks you're interested in to monitor them over time

- **Do Your Research**: This app provides information; use it alongside other financial resources
- **Diversify**: Don't focus on just a few stocks

- **Stay Updated**: Market data updates regularly; check frequently for current prices
- **Disclaimer**: Always consult with a financial advisor before making investment decisions

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Search Returns No Results**
- Ensure you've entered a valid stock symbol or company name
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different search term

**Watchlist Not Saving**
- Clear your browser cache and try again
- Ensure you have local storage enabled in your browser settings
- Check that cookies are not disabled

**Stock Data Not Loading**
- Verify you have an active internet connection
- Refresh the page and try again
- Check if the stock symbol is valid
